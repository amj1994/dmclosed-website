# PRODUCT.md — DMClosed

**Register:** brand (marketing landing page — design IS the product).

## What it is
DMClosed deploys a done-for-you **AI sales agent** for businesses. It lives inside the
channels prospects already use — WhatsApp, Instagram, Messenger, Telegram, SMS, iMessage,
email, web chat — replies in seconds, qualifies leads, handles objections, follows up for
days, and books calls straight onto the team's calendar. 24/7, no humans required to babysit.

Built on a white-labeled conversational-AI platform (DMChamp engine), operated and tuned
by DMClosed as a managed service. The customer never sees the underlying platform — they
get a branded agent that closes.

## Who it's for (B2B only)
Businesses with inbound DM/lead volume they can't answer fast enough: coaching & info
businesses, agencies, high-ticket service firms, e-commerce/DTC, real estate, med-spa /
clinics, B2B SaaS sales teams. The buyer is an owner / head of sales / marketing lead.
**Not** consumers. Copy speaks to operators and revenue, never to end-consumers.

## The promise
Speed-to-lead is the whole game. Most replies die in the inbox; DMClosed answers in
seconds, every time, and turns conversations into booked calls and closed deals.

## Pricing (two service tiers)
- **Launch — $497/mo** — one primary channel, AI agent set up, trained on the business's
  offer, objection handling, calendar booking, weekly tuning.
- **Scale — $897/mo** — all channels, outbound + comment-to-DM campaigns, CRM/GoHighLevel
  integration, priority tuning, conversation analytics, dedicated strategist.
(No public free trial — it's a managed engagement. CTA = "Book a demo".)

## Proof points (reframe, B2B)
- Closed a $40,000 deal entirely in chat; proven at $50K+ deal sizes.
- Replies in seconds, 24/7; remembers full conversation history per contact.
- One-click conversation optimization lifts conversion over time.

## Voice
Operator-to-operator. Confident, direct, a little cinematic. Revenue language, not
chatbot-feature language. "It's not a chatbot. It's an AI sales agent."

## Visual direction
Orange-drench on near-black, cinematic and kinetic. Hero centerpiece is a sleek humanoid
robot turning its head (looping video, orange-graded). THREE.js ambient field + GSAP
choreography throughout. Reference mood: a dark AI-product hero with a single committed
incandescent-orange identity. See DESIGN.md.
